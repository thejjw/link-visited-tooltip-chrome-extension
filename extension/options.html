Domain Exclusions

Add domains where you don't want the tooltip to appear. Use specific domains like "apps.domain.com" to exclude only that domain, or use "domain.com" to exclude all subdomains. Domain exclusions work without visual indicators on the extension icon. (due to no "tabs" permission)

Excluded Domains

No domains excluded

Pattern Examples

  • example.com - Excludes example.com and all subdomains (a.example.com, b.example.com, etc.)
  • app.example.com - Excludes app.example.com and its subdomains, but won't block example.com
